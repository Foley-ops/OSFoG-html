Every member is expected to follow these rules to ensure our community remains welcoming and respectful:

  1. No Begging or Harassment: Do not beg or DM clan members for free items, money, or any form of assistance. Harassment of any kind will not be tolerated.
  2. Respect and Courtesy: Treat all members with kindness and respect, and avoid offensive language and behavior.
  3. No Spamming: Keep our conversations clean and easy to follow. Please do not disrupt discussions with irrelevant or repetitive messages.
  4. No Macroing or Automation: Third-party software or automation of any kind is strictly forbidden. Fair play is expected at all times.
  5. No Deception: Honesty and integrity are crucial. Do not trick, lure, or otherwise deceive people.
  6. No Unwanted Advertising: Sharing or advertising harmful, indecent, mature content, or competing addresses or handles is not allowed.
  7. Discord and Runescape Name Consistency: Your Discord name must match your Runescape Name. Impersonation will not be tolerated.
  8. Respect Privacy: Everyone has the right to their privacy. Do not reveal personal information about others without their explicit permission.
  9. Content Posting Guidelines: Ensure all posted content is appropriate and relevant. Offensive, disturbing or NSFW content is not permitted.
  10. No Politics or Controversial Topics: To maintain a friendly environment, avoid heated debates. This server is for enjoying and discussing Runescape.
  11. Report Violations: If you notice any rule violations, report them to a moderator by submitting a ticket in the "clan-support" channel.
  12. Moderator Decisions: Respect the decisions made by the moderators. If you disagree with a decision, please reach out to them privately.
  13. No Reinvites After Ban: If a member is banned, they are not allowed to be reinvited, even on an alternate account. Attempts to circumvent a ban will result in additional penalties.
